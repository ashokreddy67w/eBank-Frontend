Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I view my transaction history?

Log in to your account, go to the "Transaction History" section, and view all your past transactions.

What should I do if a transaction fails?

Ensure the recipient's details are correct and check your account balance. If the issue persists, contact support.
